My Teaching Philosophy: The "i to i + 1" Approach

Learning complex math and science shouldn't feel like hitting a brick wall. My entire teaching framework is built around the "i to i + 1" philosophy.

Here, "i" represents your exact current level of understanding. It truly does not matter where your starting line is today—there is absolutely zero judgment in my sessions. My sole goal is to discover your "i" and provide the exact scaffolding, patience, and targeted AI-powered practice needed to seamlessly carry you to "i + 1" (your next level of mastery). By breaking daunting subjects into these manageable, logical steps, we transform frustration into total academic confidence.
